Hippie City Bike Tours

Find Serenity in Austin's Best Natural Scenery

Beers, Bikes, Barton Springs

Austin has more entertainment options than you can shake a stick at, but I think the nicest way to see the city is on our trail network. Come and hang out like the locals do: We bake in the sun, and then we dip in the water, and then we repeat, every day, potentially forever. Come ride bikes among the trees, creeks, and wildlife.

My main tour is a laid-back cruise, down the Shoal Creek Trail, through Pease Park, over Lady Bird Lake, up Barton Creek, and to the Barton Springs Pool, where we'll have a light picnic and some cold beer. Whether it's winter or summer, you'll have the opportunity to jump in the pool for a while before we pack up and ride back uptown. This no-cars, no-worries cruise is everything I love about Austin, encapsulated in an afternoon of fun in the sun.

About Ben

I've had a lot of odd jobs. I've been a street performer, IT guy, pizza guy, waiter, cashier, software engineer, art curator, camera salesman, bong salesman, and honestly probably some other stuff. This is all just a roundabout way of saying that I'm not terriffic at holding down a normal job.

I spent a lot of time over the pandemic trying to learn python programming and other computer-boy skills. After four years of staring at the screen and beating my head against the wall, I realized all I really wanted to do was bike to the pool and smoke grass in the big Texas sun. It brings me great joy to relish in Austin's natural wonder. This company is an attempt to materialize the perfect "Austin Slacker Existence," and spend every day exploring the natural beauty of this great town.
